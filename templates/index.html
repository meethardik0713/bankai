Process

Three steps to complete financial clarity

01

Upload Documents

Upload bank statements, 26AS, AIS, invoices, or GSTR-2B data. We support all major Indian banks and GST portal formats.

02

Auto-Consolidate

Our engine scores loan eligibility, flags risk, reconciles income across sources, auto-fills GST returns, and builds Schedule III financials — work that takes hours, done in seconds.

03

Export & Decide

Download loan eligibility reports, Balance Sheet, P&L, GSTR-1/3B auto-filled data, reconciliation reports — all in clean Excel format.

Trust & Security

Your financial data stays yours. Always.

We process everything locally — no financial data sent to third-party AI APIs. No data is stored on our servers. Every analysis happens in real-time and is discarded immediately after your session.

🔒

No Data Storage

Your PDFs are processed and immediately deleted. Zero retention.

🛡️

No External AI APIs

All processing happens on our servers. Your financial data never leaves to third-party AI services.

🇮🇳

Built for India

Supports all major Indian banks, GST portal formats, 26AS, AIS, and UPI transaction patterns.

⚡

Instant Analysis

Results in under 3 seconds. No waiting, no delays.

Capabilities

Everything you need, automated

Instant Loan Eligibility

FOIR, DSCR, average balance, and estimated loan eligibility — calculated automatically for DSA and NBFC underwriting.

Credit Risk & Fraud Flags

Bounce detection, circular transactions, window dressing alerts, and compliance risk scoring built for bulk underwriting.

Bank Statement Intelligence

Upload any Indian bank PDF — get categorized transactions, ITR insights, loan assessment, audit checks, and compliance flags.

Books Finalisation

Consolidate 26AS, AIS, bank statements, and GST data into Balance Sheet & P&L in Schedule III format.

GST Compliance Suite

GSTR-1 auto-fill, GSTR-3B computation, GSTR-2A/2B ITC reconciliation, and compliance calendar — all in one place.

Excel Export Ready

Every module exports clean, formatted Excel — loan reports, BS, P&L, GSTR data, reconciliation reports — ready for handoff.
