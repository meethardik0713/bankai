The Story

How we got here

It started with a simple problem — as a trader and student, I was downloading bank statements and manually scrolling through hundreds of transactions. So I built a parser that could read any Indian bank PDF and categorize everything instantly.

Then an investor saw the potential and pushed us toward GST compliance — GSTR-1 auto-fill, GSTR-3B computation, 2A/2B reconciliation. My father, who has deep GST domain expertise, shaped how these modules actually work in practice.

The real turning point was a conversation with CA Harsh Gupta. He told me bluntly — bank statement categorization saves a CA 10% of their time. The real pain is books finalisation — clubbing 26AS, AIS, GST data, TDS returns into a final Balance Sheet and P&L in Schedule III format. That's where CAs spend hours.

So we built the Multi-Source Financial Consolidator — upload your bank statement, 26AS, and AIS together, and get reconciled financials instantly. No data sent to external AI APIs. Everything processed locally.

We incorporated as AarogyamFin Financial Services Pvt. Ltd., applied for DPIIT recognition — and the Government of India validated what we'd been building. Certificate No. DIPP265941. Finance Technology. Business Finance. That's not just a badge. That's the government saying this problem is real and our approach works.

Government Recognized

The Government of India recognizes what we're building.

AarogyamFin Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. has been officially recognized as a startup by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India.

This recognition is not handed out to just anyone. DPIIT evaluates startups on their innovation, scalability, and potential to solve real problems in the Indian economy. Out of the thousands of applications they receive, only those building something genuinely impactful make the cut.

For AarogyamFin, the recognition came under Finance Technology industry and Business Finance sector — a direct acknowledgement that the problem we're solving — automating CA workflows, GST compliance, and books finalisation — is a real, validated need in India's financial ecosystem.

When a CA or business owner uses AarogyamFin, they're not trusting a random tool built in someone's bedroom. They're trusting a government-recognized fintech company that has been vetted, registered, and certified by the same department that oversees India's startup ecosystem.
